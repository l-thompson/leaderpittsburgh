Common questions

What you need to know

Where does the data come from?

All permits are sourced from the City of Pittsburgh's public data portal (WPRDC). We pull and process the data daily — nothing manual, nothing scraped.

What does "unassigned" mean?

When a permit is filed without a licensed contractor on record, it shows as unassigned. These are the highest-priority leads — the job exists, but no one has it yet.

How does the ML forecast work?

We run a Poisson regression model on 12 months of permit history, weighted toward recent activity. It outputs predicted permit volume per neighborhood for the next 4 weeks.

What if I want to cancel?

Cancel anytime from the email unsubscribe link or by replying to any report. No hoops, no calls. Your billing stops at the end of the current period.

Do I need software or a login?

No. You get an email with a PDF attached every morning. Open it like any other email. Nothing to install, no dashboard to check.

Is this only for big companies?

No. A single contractor who lands one extra job per month from these leads more than covers the cost. Most of our target subscribers are owner-operators and small teams.
